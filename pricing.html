Pricing questions

Frequently asked

What happens when included hours are used?

FutureProof will notify the authorized contact and request approval before material overage work. Approved additional time is normally billed at $125/hour.

Are software subscriptions included?

No. Microsoft, Google, backup, security, domain, hosting, and other third-party costs remain separate unless a written quote specifically bundles them.

Do unused hours carry forward?

No, not by default. A retainer reserves recurring monthly capacity. Rollover applies only when written into the service schedule.

Can a plan be customized?

Yes. User counts, devices, response expectations, onsite work, projects, and recurring activities can be defined in a custom service schedule.
